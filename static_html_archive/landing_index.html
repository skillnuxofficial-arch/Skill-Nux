Privacy & Terms

1. Information We Collect

We collect name, email, phone, college ID, and skill details during registration. For businesses, we also collect GST and visiting card uploads for verification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to verify identity, match you with projects or talent, send notifications, and improve our services. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Security

All sensitive data is encrypted. College ID and Aadhaar/PAN uploads are used only for one-time verification. Payment data is handled via secure escrow — we never store card details.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to keep you logged in and understand platform usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work correctly.

5. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and data anytime by emailing skillnux.official@gmail.com. We will process your request within 7 business days.

1. Acceptance of Terms

By creating an account on SkillNux, you agree to these Terms & Conditions. If you do not agree, please do not use the platform.

2. Student & Freelancer Conduct

Students must submit honest skill test responses and accurate college information. Fake verifications result in permanent suspension.

3. Business Conduct

Businesses must provide clear project descriptions. Misrepresentation of scope after assignment is a violation. Businesses agree to release escrow within 3 days of project delivery.

4. Project Matching

SkillNux's matching algorithm is final. Businesses cannot request specific students by name during initial assignment. Favouriting and rehiring after a successful project is allowed.

5. Payments & Escrow

All payments are held in escrow until project completion is confirmed. SkillNux charges a platform fee of 15% on completed transactions. Disputes are handled within 5 business days.

1. Project Cancellation Before Work Begins

If a project is cancelled before any work has started, the full amount is refunded to the business within 5–7 business days.

2. Partial Completion

If a project is partially completed and both parties agree on partial payment, SkillNux facilitates the agreed split. Platform fees are non-refundable in partial completion cases.

3. Quality Disputes

If a business raises a quality dispute within 48 hours of delivery, our team reviews the work against the original brief. If valid, a revision or partial refund is arranged.

4. Platform Fee

The SkillNux platform fee (15%) is non-refundable once a project has been assigned and work has commenced.
